# Privacy Policy

Deposit Reminder works completely offline.

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

All deposit records, reminder settings, language preferences, and app lock settings are stored locally on your device only.

The app does not require account registration and does not connect to external servers.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection tools.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored app data will be removed from your device.
