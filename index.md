# Deposit Reminder

A private and offline deposit maturity reminder app.

## Never miss a deposit maturity date

Deposit Reminder helps you track fixed-term deposits and receive timely reminders before maturity.

## Key Features

* Track deposit maturity dates
* Local notifications
* Face ID / Device Passcode protection
* Multiple language support
* Works completely offline
* No account required
* No ads
* No data collection

## Privacy First

Your financial information stays on your device.

Deposit Reminder does not:

* Collect personal information
* Upload data to cloud services
* Connect to bank accounts
* Track user activity
